Privacy Policy for Pomodoro Ice Timer

Effective Date: December 1, 2025

Data Collection

Pomodoro Ice Timer does not collect, transmit, or sell any user data. We do not have servers, and we do not use analytics software.

Local Storage

All data (including timer settings, focus history, and "frozen" site lists) is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. This data never leaves your browser.

Permissions Usage

We require permission to access website URLs (Host Permissions) solely for the functionality of the "Site Blocking" feature. This is performed locally to check if a site matches your blocklist. We do not track or store your browsing history.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact the developer via the support link on the Chrome Web Store.
